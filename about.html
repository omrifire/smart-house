About Us

Welcome to IoT House! We are two passionate students, Omri Firestain and Ido Jan, working on our final project in electronics.

Our smart home system allows users to control various electronic devices such as fan, entry gate, curtain, door lock, and light that adjust to external brightness levels. Additionally, we have implemented an alert system with a buzzer for motion detection in the living room and a distance sensor for outdoor monitoring.

Our goal is to create a seamless and efficient smart home experience using advanced IoT technologies.
